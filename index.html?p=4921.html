The Future

Back to Portfolio

MBT, a gaming community which was officially established on 15th September 2006, by a group of aspiring Malaysian gamers focusing on the “Battlefield” franchise. With over a decade of experience, MBT has grown into a full-fledged community, run by working professionals and young adults, fueled by passion in gaming.

 

As the new era and next generation of gaming approaches, we’re making our move to making MBT greater by introducing several changes led by our next generation in leadership – with the same core values as before.

As MBT approaches its 12th year anniversary, we’re presenting a new look, to better represent with the next generation. Based on same core values set by our founding fathers, MBT is ushering into a new era led by a fresh committee of young gamers focusing on creating a positive gaming community & providing opportunities to those who wish to strive further in competitive gaming or content creation.MBT’s vision, from then till now, we continue to strive in building and growing a diverse community of gamers. MBT has decided to move its official communications server to Discord to introduce a more diverse platform for the public to play and engage with our community member, with a diverse range of online games such as PUBG, Overwatch, Fornite, DOTA, League of Legends, Mobile Legends Bang Bang and other upcoming online games.In addition to gaming, MBT will encourage and promote the growth of more streamers to further improve our public social media outreach. MBT will introduce collaboration with brands and other communities for our content creators, building individual personalities and instill confidence in our streamers.
