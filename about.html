Our Story

MBT (Malaysian Battlefield Team) is a gaming organization founded and based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia since 2006. MBT was founded with the foundation of brotherhood and community-driven gaming.

For over 18 years, we have been at the forefront of competitive gaming in Southeast Asia, building teams, hosting events, and fostering a community of passionate gamers who share our values of teamwork, dedication, and sportsmanship.

Our Mission

To create and nurture a positive gaming community that brings together players from all backgrounds. We believe in providing opportunities for gamers to strive further in competitive gaming and content creation.
